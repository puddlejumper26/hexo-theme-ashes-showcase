About this site

This theme is inspired by the work Ashes and Snow of Mr Gregory Colbert. His art work brings me with minds of peace and the connections among human and nature. Pictures used in this theme are from his work.

This website is completely built with Hexo, based on the theme landfarz. Redeveloped by Puddlejumper26

  • HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Ejs, Stylus,YAML, Git
  • some ejs codes are modified
  • some stylus codes are modified
  • pagination is added
  • clock is added
